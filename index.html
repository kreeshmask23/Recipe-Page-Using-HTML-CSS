Grandma's Creamy Chicken Pasta

This Creamy Chicken Pasta recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 butter and cream
  • 2 optional flours
  • 2 to 3 pieces of your favoured veggies

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken in half lengthwise to create 2-4 thinner slices. Tenderize with a meat mallet.
  2. Season each side and dredge it in flour. Sear in olive oil and set aside.
  3. Deglaze the skillet with white wine and reduce by half.Add butter and garlic.
  4. Add flour and stir continuously. Add half and half, chicken broth, and seasonings in small splashes.
  5. Stir in the cheese over low heat, then add the pasta, followed by the chicken. Stir to combine.
